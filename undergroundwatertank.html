Tank Specifications

  • Tanks from 2,000 litres to 60,000 litres with diameters from 4 ft to 8 ft.
  • Can be installed in a few hours and used the same day.
  • Manufactured and tested as per international standards (AWWA D 120) for leak-proof storage and protection from contamination.
  • Designed for a minimum life of 50 years.
  • No contact with steel in wetted parts – no corrosion.
  • Overhead & aboveground tanks are protected from UV rays.
  • Installation space is hardly 5% more than the volume of water stored.
  • Can be shifted from one place to another, even after years of usage.
  • Glossy internal finish helps eliminate growth of algae and other bio-fouling.
