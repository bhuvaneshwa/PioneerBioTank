FRP Bio-Septic Tank Installation Layout

A simple, clear explanation of how the FRP Bio-Septic Tank is installed at your site — showing the toilet line, screen chamber, FRP tank, and soak pit connection.

1. Toilet to Screen Chamber

Wastewater from your toilets first enters the **M.S. bar screen chamber**, where large particles are filtered.

2. FRP Bio-Septic Tank Placement

The FRP tank is placed on a base layer of **quarry dust**, with inlet & outlet pipes aligned for smooth flow between chambers.

3. Soak Pit / Dispersion System

Treated, odor-free water flows to a **soak pit** or recharge chamber, ensuring safe groundwater percolation.

Installation follows DRDO-based design standards used in defence, schools, hospitals, and residential projects across India.
