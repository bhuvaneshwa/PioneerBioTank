How It Works

What happens inside a Pioneer Bio-Septic Tank?

Waste enters from your toilets and bathrooms.

Solids settle in the first chamber.

Special bacteria digest waste in multiple stages.

Water gets clarified and becomes odour-free.

Treated water goes to soak pit, gardening or recharge (as per design).

The technology is based on DRDO (Gwalior) bio-septic design, used in defence and large institutions.
